#  Welcome to IT Inventry

In today’s digital-first world, managing IT assets efficiently is essential. **IT Inventry** simplifies and centralizes asset tracking covering hardware, software, licenses, and lifecycles. Designed for startups to enterprises, it ensures nothing is lost or overlooked. With modern architecture and enterprise-grade security, we help you stay compliant, audit-ready, and in control.

---

##  Key Features

-  **Complete Asset Tracking**: Track hardware, software, licenses, and digital assets from acquisition to decommission.
-  **Enterprise-Grade Security**: Ensure sensitive IT data is safe with modern security protocols and compliance standards.
-  **Real-Time Reporting & Audits**: Generate instant reports and stay always audit-ready.
-  **License & Warranty Management**: Stay ahead of renewals, warranty expiries, and license allocations.
-  **Customizable Inventory Categories**: Tailor your asset categories to match your company’s structure.
-  **Modern, Accessible UI**: Clean, intuitive, and responsive design built with **Next.js**, **Framer Motion**, and **Acertinity**.

---

##  How It Works

1. **Sign Up or Log In**: Create your secure admin account.
2. **Add and Categorize Assets**: Log hardware, software, licenses, and infrastructure.
3. **Track and Manage Lifecycles**: Monitor asset status, warranties, and license validity.
4. **Run Real-Time Reports**: Generate compliance reports and inventory audits instantly.
5. **Stay Compliant and Secure**: Use built-in tools to ensure industry-grade security and data safety.

---

##  Technologies Behind IT Inventry

- **Next.js**: Scalable full-stack framework for blazing-fast apps.
- **Tailwind CSS**: Rapid, utility-first styling for responsive UI.
- **Framer Motion**: Smooth, modern animations and interactions.
- **Acertinity Design System**: Clean, consistent UI components for enterprise-grade dashboards.
- **PostgreSQL (NeonDB)**: Cloud-native, serverless relational database.
- **Prisma ORM**: Type-safe, easy-to-use database operations.
- **Clerk Authentication**: Secure, modern auth infrastructure.
- **React Hook Form & React Query**: Smooth forms and efficient data fetching.
- **Sentry**: Application performance monitoring and error tracking.
